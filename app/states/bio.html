"Expect raw, glitchy blues-rock thick with heavy guitar rifts, synths and samples."


Ulysses Wells is set to have an exciting 2016. Fresh off the back of the success of his first mini-tour around the UK, he and his live band played their first London showcase at the Islington 02 in March.

Born and raised in Warwickshire, Ulysses has spent years refining his own style of electronic blues-rock. Last spring he began recording his first solo EP with producer John Robertson and is releasing his debut track 'Gruffalo' this summer.
