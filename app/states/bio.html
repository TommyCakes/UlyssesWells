Young British electronic blues-rock musician Ulysses Wells is set to have an exciting 2016. Fresh off the back of the success of his first mini-tour around the UK in February, Ulysses is playing his first London show at the Islington 02 in March.
Born and raised in Warwickshire, Ulysses has spent the last six months recording his first solo EP with John Robertson, producer to Grace Jones and Rihanna. He and his band are looking forward to a summer full of festivals and further opportunities to record and play live.

Expect raw, glitchy blues-rock thick with heavy guitar rifts, synths and samples.
