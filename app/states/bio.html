Ulysses Wells is set to have an exciting 2016. Fresh off the back of the success of his first mini-tour around the UK in February, he and his live band are playing their first London showcase at the Islington 02 on March 18th.

Born and raised in Warwickshire, Ulysses has spent years refining his own style of electronic blues-rock. In June 2015 he began recording his first solo EP with producer John Robertson. Ulysses is looking forward to a summer full of festivals and further opportunities to record and play live.

Expect raw, glitchy blues-rock thick with heavy guitar rifts, synths and samples.
